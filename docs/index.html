About Us

We are a Startup Vtuber Agency based in Singapore, managed by L&C Digital LLP. This agency was started by two like-minded individuals with passion in anime, gaming and streaming.

We plan to reach out to the global audience with the goal of putting smiles on people's faces with entertaining livestreams.

We are currently looking for potential talent to take on the role of our pillar Vtuber.

Recruitment

As a startup Vtuber agency, we understand that we do not have the reach of agencies such as Hololive or Nijisanji, but we will invest resources to market and nurture this project to the best of our abilities. Hence, we are looking for someone who is willing to grow together with us.

As our planned Live2D model is female, we will only be recruiting females, unless the talent can demonstrate the ability to mimic a female voice over long periods of time.

The benefits that will be provided to the recruited talent will be displayed in the following section

Why Join Us?

  • Paid Role

    We will compensate you with an undisclosed monthly base salary that will be paid weekly, provided responsibilities for the week are fulfilled. In addition to a dynamic commission system of revenue from the streaming platform paid monthly.

  • Streaming Resources

    You will be provided with an XLR microphone and audio interface, as well as a webcam and second monitor if required. Upgrading of PC if deemed required, character BGM, stream overlays/backgrounds etc provided.

  • High Quality Live2D Model

    We will provide you with a high quality Live2D model, illustrated by a reputable artist.

  • High Degree of Freedom

    You are free to set your own schedule according to your needs, as long as you are able to commit at least 3 streams a week.

  • Marketing

    We will spend time and effort marketing your content across different platforms. We will also help you to organise collaborations with other online personalities.

  • Games Provided

    In the scenario where you do not have the games that you would like to stream, we will purchase said games, up to 2 games per month.
